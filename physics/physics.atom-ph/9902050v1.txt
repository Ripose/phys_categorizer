Abstract

Closure relations satisﬁed by the radial Dirac-Coulomb Sturmians are proved analytically.
The Sturmian expansion of the Dirac-Coulomb Green function is transformed to the form
containing only series with summations running over non-negative indices. The main paper
was published in J. Phys. B: At. Mol. Opt. Phys. 30 (1997) 825–61 [Erratum: 30 (1997)
2747], see also J. Phys. A: Math. Gen. 31 (1998) 4963–90 [Erratum: 31 (1998) 7415–6].
