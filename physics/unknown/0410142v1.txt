ABSTRACT 

Temporal evolution of the inhomogeneous energy density driven 

instability (IEDDI) is examined in presence of a nonuniform transverse dc 
electric field in  collisional magnetized plasma cylinder. Using experimentally 
known parameters relevant to IEDDI, we have estimated the growth rate for 
n =1, 2 and 3 nonlocal eigenmodes.

case of small vde and large vE, the EIC waves are driven by the transverse, 
localized, dc electric field (IEDD Instability)3-4.
